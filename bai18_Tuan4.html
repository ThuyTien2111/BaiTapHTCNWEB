Many of us may have become accustomed to mailing lists, and would like a similar workflow. To do this:

- Click on your avatar in the upper-right
- Click “Preferences”
- Scroll down to the “Email” section
- Check everything, including and especially “Receive email notifications and email digests even if you are active on the forum.”
